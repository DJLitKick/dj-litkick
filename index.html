The Artist

Munich's Most
Electrifying Set

DJ LitKick emerged from Bavaria's nightlife scene to conquer dance floors across Europe. Winner of the German DJ Contest 2020 — the highest recognition of skill in Germany's DJ landscape. Performing in Munich, Kaiserslautern, Switzerland, Croatia, and beyond.

His sets are built on three pillars: razor-sharp scratching, seamless transitions, and magnetic microphone energy. Every night is a choreographed experience — from the first beat drop to the last note.

"Not just a DJ… an Entertainer."
